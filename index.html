Welcome to Discovery Library!

Dear colleagues, welcome to our website! It was developed exclusively for Discovery staff. Here you can find all the necessary information for your work in our clubs as well as additional resources you might find useful. Don't hesitate to message our Academic and Clients Departments if you have any questions or suggestions. We would be glad if you join us on social media!
